About HiloCurado


HiloCurado is a concept of Dr. VipinK RP, who is scientist in the Instrumentation field of Remote Sensing, Space Technology, Electrical Vehicles and Smart Home and Industry Automation.
Dr. VipinK R P is a Automotive / UAV(ARINC-429/664) Embedded Systems | QT/Visual Studio/Visual Code | C/C++, HTML, CSS | Bare-Metal | OTA | Firmware/RTOS/Linux Device Driver Developer | Eagle/Altium/KiCAD PCB Designer.

Hilo Curado is a global technology brand focused on the Internet of Things.

It offers solutions in the areas of construction, Home automation, Industrial automation, Pay2Use Devices for vending machines, Agricultural automation systems, information technology, and WFH infrastructure.

The factory of HiloCurado is located in Pune, India.

In addition, we offer OEM/ODM and brand distribution services.

The HiloCurado product development is both elegant and gorgeous, and it was made to fit into any home's decor.

We design and build all of the smart home gadgets ourselves, and we pay attention to even the tiniest details of the project.


Our Values


MISSION: We achieve our vision by assisting our clients in achieving their business objectives. We assist them throughout the product development lifecycle, from prototype to volume manufacturing. We personalise our production process to the demands of our clients, using our deep experience and unwavering dedication to quality, great customer service, and dependability. We are a real extension of our customers' teams, resulting in a mutually beneficial growth collaboration.


VISION: The HiloCurado network considers it its purpose to contribute to the improvement of business and public policies and practises relating to the protection and respect of human rights and the environment in the global electronics supply chain, with a particular emphasis on major brand firms.

HiloCurado is a strong supporter of contractually enforceable government regulations governing workers' rights, corporate accountability, and environmental protection, as well as their implementation in accordance with internationally agreed human rights and sustainability standards, including strict adherence to International labour organization labour conventions.

The HiloCurado alliance envisions civil society organisations as playing a critical role in mobilising and informing workers at the grassroots level, monitoring and recording business and governmental behaviour, and participating in improvement initiatives. Strong, autonomous trade unions should play an important role in social discussion and collective bargaining, as well as in mature trade union-employer relations. It is critical to educate workers on their rights and responsibilities.
